Visa Guidance

As a student traveling to a foreign country to study, you will need to be issued a valid visa that indicates the duration for which you are allowed to stay in the country. Since the time taken to process visa applications varies from country to country, we recommend that you apply for a student visa as soon as you have received an offer of admission from your chosen university, regardless of the start date of your program. The earlier you apply, the safer you are from any possible problems that might occur during the process.

Different countries have different visa requirements, and it is always recommended to consult with your education counselor or the embassy of the country you are planning to travel to. The most common visa documentation includes:

- Proof of Admission

- Valid Passport

- Proof of Sufficient Funds To Meet Expenses

- Proof of Proficiency In English

- Passport Photographs

At Envision Overseas, we work with our students through this process, advising them on the different stages and how to progress through each one. By staying updated with all the recent changes to university and visa application procedures, we reduce your burden of having to do so. In addition, we help you prepare all your documents for submission, as well as translating, certifying, and helping you book appointments for the visa interviews.
